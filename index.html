100% Free Outreach & Rehabilitation

Neuro-Care Outreach Trust (NCOT)

Bridging the Gap in Neurodevelopmental & Psychiatric Care

A comprehensive community-centered neuro-rehabilitation and outreach initiative dedicated to children and adults living with IDDs, neurodevelopmental disorders, and cognitive impairments in Karachi[span_0](start_span)[span_0](end_span).

Our Vision & Commitment

The ecosystem for intellectual and developmental disabilities (IDDs) in Karachi is critically fragmented and financially out of reach for marginalized populations[span_1](start_span)[span_1](end_span).

"Every diagnostic evaluation, therapy session, medication, counseling service, rehabilitation intervention, and caregiver support program provided through NCOT shall be delivered completely free of cost to beneficiaries[span_2](start_span)[span_2](end_span)."

Our Core Objectives

I. Early Identification & Pediatric Rehab

Focused on pediatric neuro-psychiatric assessment, speech and language therapy, occupational therapy, sensory integration, and physical rehab for ages 0–12 during key neuroplasticity windows[span_3](start_span)[span_3](end_span).

II. Transitional Adult Services

Providing behavioral support, puberty/hygiene management, caregiver training, and dignity-centered adult vocational and skill-development workshops for individuals aged 12 and above[span_4](start_span)[span_4](end_span).

III. Community-Based Outreach

Eliminating geographical and financial transportation barriers through localized mobile outreach rehabilitation units, therapy camps, and peripheral-area screening drives[span_5](start_span)[span_5](end_span).

IV. Integrated Neuro-Psychiatric Support

Functioning as a coordination hub for chronic neuropsychiatric and anti-epileptic medication support, alongside crisis management protocols and local physician tele-guidance[span_6](start_span)[span_6](end_span).

V. Family & Caregiver Support

Addressing continuous caregiving burnout by offering structured respite care hours, maternal wellness initiatives, sibling peer-support, and holistic family psychoeducation[span_7](start_span)[span_7](end_span).
